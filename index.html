Many people with epilepsy say that the freedom of art helps them to express their experiences of seizures. Accordingly, the photography is a powerful communication tool for experiences that are hard to explain in words. Through this series of photographs, I wanted to show off how is it to be affected by epilepsy and to raise the awareness about this condition, which I have. This work helped me to achieve greater self awareness about my relationship with epilepsy and cope with all the emotions associated with it. Through this photographs, I tried to visualize the stages of seizures and the uncertainties that come with epilepsy.
